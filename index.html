What Is Voidling Bound?

A sci-fi blend of monster taming and third-person action

Voidling Bound is a third-person shooter meets creature collector developed by Hatchery Games. You play as a Space Wrangler who uses neural-link technology to directly control alien creatures called Voidlings.

Launching June 9, 2026 on Steam, the game features:

  • 8 base species, 248+ total evolutions — 31 evolution branches per species
  • Deep breeding system — cross-breed Voidlings to inherit stats and traits
  • Gene splicing — mix and match 47+ gene fragments to create custom voidlings
  • Fast-paced combat — shooting, slashing, dodging, parrying, and ultimate abilities
  • 5 attribute system — Strength, Vitality, Essence, Recuperation, Agility
  • Endless Abyss mode — infinite challenge with escalating rewards
