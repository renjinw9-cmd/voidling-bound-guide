📊 Voidling Bound Attributes: Complete Guide

📅 Updated: June 8, 2026🎮 Core Mechanics⏱️ ~5 min read

The 5 Attribute System

Every time a Voidling levels up, you gain attribute points to allocate across 5 core stats. Customize each Voidling's playstyle—glass cannon, tank, or anything in between. Confirmed from the official Steam store page.

AttributeRoleBest For
💪 StrengthPhysical damage outputMelee Voidlings, aggressive builds
🛡️ VitalityHealth pool & survivabilityTanks, Abyss deep runs
✨ EssenceSpecial ability power & energyAbility-spam, elemental damage
🔄 RecuperationHealth & energy regen speedSustain builds, long missions
⚡ AgilityMovement speed, dodge, attack speedHit-and-run, evasive playstyles

📝 Source Note

Attribute names confirmed from official Steam description. Exact numerical scaling per point will be verified after the June 9 launch.

How Points Work

Voidlings earn XP from missions, combat, and Training Room—level up, earn points, allocate freely.

💡 Early Game

Spread points across 2-3 attributes rather than min-maxing. Stay versatile while learning enemy types.

System Synergies

  • Nature system: Natures add bonus stats on top of allocated points
  • Breeding: High-stat parents produce offspring with better base stats
  • Splicing Station: Transfer stat-boosting genes from donor Voidlings
  • Corrupted DNA: Abyss catalyzers push stats beyond normal limits
