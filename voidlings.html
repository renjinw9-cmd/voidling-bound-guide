    🔥 Fire Element Voidlings

    Fire Voidlings specialize in high burst damage and DoT (Damage over Time) burn effects. Strong against Ice and Toxic enemies.

    Infernaut

    FireStarter

    Ability: Flame Burst — fires explosive fireballs that leave burning ground

    Obtain: Starting Voidling (choose during tutorial)

    Evolves to: Pyroclasm (DPS) or Magma Guard (Tank)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    Scorchling

    FireEarly Game

    Ability: Heat Wave — close-range AoE pushback with burn

    Obtain: Volcanic zones, early game capture

    Evolves to: Scorchbeast (ranged) or Emberclaw (melee)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    Krakatoa

    FireRare

    Ability: Eruption — massive delayed AoE explosion

    Obtain: Boss drop or rare volcanic spawn

    Evolves to: Supernova (ultimate DPS) or Caldera (fortress tank)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    ❄️ Ice Element Voidlings

    Ice Voidlings excel at crowd control — slowing, freezing, and controlling the battlefield. Strong against Fire and Electric.

    Gelik

    IceStarter

    Ability: Frost Slam — melee AoE with ice damage and freeze buildup

    Obtain: Starting Voidling (choose during tutorial)

    Evolves to: Cryostrider (mobility) or Glacius (pure CC)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    Snowsling

    IceEarly Game

    Ability: Snow Sling — ranged ice projectile that stacks freeze

    Obtain: Frozen regions, evolved from certain early game Voidlings

    Evolves to: Blizzardon (AoE) or Frostfang (single target DPS)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    ⚡ Electric Element Voidlings

    Electric Voidlings are fast, agile, and deal chain lightning damage. Strong against Void and Toxic enemies.

    Voltra

    ElectricEarly Game

    Ability: Chain Lightning — bounces between nearby enemies

    Obtain: Tech facility zones, early-mid game capture

    Evolves to: Stormcaller (AoE chain) or Tesladon (single-target burst)

    📝 Stats pending full release

    ☠️ Toxic Element Voidlings

    Toxic Voidlings specialize in poison, debuffs, and sustained damage over time. Strong against Electric.

    Kerapin

    ToxicMid Game

    Ability: Burst Rifle — shoots toxic rounds, evolves into Bolla Blast (knockback) or Snow Sling (freeze hybrid)

    Obtain: Swamp/industrial zones
