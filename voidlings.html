🐉 Voidling Bound: Confirmed Voidlings

📅 Updated: June 10, 2026 🐉 9 base species (Steam) ⏱️ Reference Page

📑 On This Page

    ⚠️ Launch Week — Incomplete Data

    Last verified: June 2026 launch build. Voidling Bound launched June 9, 2026. The Steam page confirms 9 base species. We have confirmed names and details for 5 from public sources (Steam demo, developer interviews, press previews). The remaining species will be added as they are publicly confirmed. ✅ = confirmed from a named public source.

    What We Know About the Roster

    Voidling Bound has 9 base species (Steam store page). Each species has 31 evolution branches (RPGamer developer interview with Creative Director Jonathan Rancourt). The full game offers ~62 species choices through branching evolution, plus near-limitless combinations through the Splicing Station.

    The free Steam demo includes 2 playable Voidlings (Kwipeck and Glick). The remaining 7 base species are in the full game. We are documenting them as players discover and publicly confirm each one.

    Note: During an earlier playtest, 8 species with 248 evolution forms were available. The full launch has 9 species. Some older articles and videos reference the playtest numbers — they are not wrong, just outdated.

    ✅ Confirmed Voidlings

    These Voidlings have been confirmed through the free Steam demo, developer interviews (Jonathan Rancourt, Hatchery Games), and hands-on press previews. Each is marked with its source.

    Kwipeck 🐔 ✓ Confirmed (Demo)

    Neutral (base)Starter

    Primary: Beak Minigun — long-range rapid-fire burst

    Secondary: Explosive bomb

    Playstyle: Nimble long-range DPS. Fast dodge, responsive shooter feel. Keep distance, strafe, fire.

    Evolves to: Poison-sniper path (Toxic element) via mutagen

    Variant: Mutated Kwipeck — hatches from Golden Egg with pre-evolved stats and extra jump

    📝 Source: Steam Demo, Pixelkin preview

    Glick 🦎 ✓ Confirmed (Demo)

    Neutral (base)Starter

    Primary: Cone Blast — close-range shotgun spread

    Secondary: Leap attack (gap closer)

    Playstyle: Aggressive short-range brawler. Close distance, blast, reposition. Higher risk, higher burst.

    Evolves to: Arctic Glick (Cryo path) — freeze + shatter, effective vs robotic enemies

    📝 Source: Steam Demo, Pixelkin preview

    Kerapin ✓ Confirmed (Dev Interview)

    ToxicMid Game

    Primary: Burst Rifle — rapid-fire ranged attacks

    Evolves to: Bolla Blast (knockback-focused) or Snow Sling (ice projectiles — explode & freeze when stacked)

    Obtain: Full game (not in demo). Specific location to be confirmed by community.

    📝 Source: RPGamer developer interview (Jonathan Rancourt, April 2026)

    Nimiod 🕊️ ✓ Confirmed (Dev Interview)

    Element TBCMid Game

    Ability: Hover — sustained aerial mobility

    Playstyle: Aerial melee. Requires special enemy design to accommodate hovering — unique among Voidlings.

    Obtain: Full game (not in demo). Specific location to be confirmed by community.

    📝 Source: RPGamer developer interview (April 2026)

    Gwigoon 🧬 ✓ Confirmed (Spliced)

    SplicedEndgame

    Origin: Gene-crafted — created via Splicing Station from multiple gene sources. Not found in the wild.

    Obtain: Splicing Station (endgame system). Requires collecting specific gene fragments across multiple Voidlings.

    📝 Confirmed spliced Voidling. See Evolution & Breeding Guide for Splicing Station details.
