🧬 Voidling Bound Splicing Station: Gene Crafting Guide

📅 Updated: June 8, 2026🎮 Core Mechanics⏱️ ~5 min read

What Is the Splicing Station?

The Splicing Station is Voidling Bound's endgame customization system. Using genes collected throughout your journey, craft unique Voidlings that combine strengths from all your discoveries—unlocking synergies "otherwise impossible to overcome ever-increasing challenges" (official Steam description).

🧬 Official Description

"Acquire all Voidlings, collect body parts, colors, and eye genes to customize your specimens. Acquire game-changing ability genes and mutated perks to fill your collection. Use your gene collection to craft unique Voidlings."

What You Can Do

  • Custom appearance: mix body parts, colors, and eye genes
  • Ability transfer: move powerful ability genes between Voidlings
  • Stat inheritance: carry over strong stats from donor Voidlings
  • Element fusion: create dual-element Voidlings
  • Synergy unlocks: specific gene combos trigger special bonuses

Gene Collection Sources

  1. Hatching Voidlings with desirable traits
  2. Completing missions and arena challenges
  3. Deep Abyss runs (rare genes)
  4. Breeding for specific gene inheritance

⚠️ Donor Voidlings Are Consumed

Splicing permanently consumes the donor. Never splice your only copy of a rare Voidling. Plan your craft before committing resources.

Confirmed Voidling: Gwigoon

Revealed alongside the Splicing Station in the Monster Taming Direct 2025 trailer: Gwigoon is a "mischievous master of self-replication that wields its clones in combat for devastating effects." This clone ability makes Gwigoon a prime candidate for gene transfer via splicing.

Splicing Strategy

  • Plan before splicing: identify target gene combos before spending resources
  • Priority order: high-value abilities → optimal stats → cosmetics
  • Abyss synergy: splicing is critical for deep Abyss runs where synergy bonuses matter
  • Breed first, splice later: get a strong base through breeding, then enhance via splicing
