🧪 Voidling Bound Breeding & Splicing Guide

📅 Updated: June 8, 2026 🧬 Advanced ⏱️ 10 min read

📑 On This Page

    Breeding Basics: How It Works

    Once you unlock the Breeding Station in your ship, you can pair one male and one female Voidling to produce an egg. The offspring inherits stats, traits, and natures from both parents — this is how you create genetically superior Voidlings.

    What Gets Inherited

    TraitInheritance RuleStrategy
    🐉 SpeciesOffspring is same species as motherMale can be different species (for certain gene transfers)
    📊 Base StatsAverage of parents + small random varianceBreed two high-stat Voidlings to push stat ceiling

    The Nature System: Quality Matters

    When a Voidling hatches from an egg, it may roll a Nature — a permanent stat bonus. There are three quality tiers:

    💡 Breeding for Perfect Natures

    Golden Eggs and Silver Eggs have higher base Nature quality rates. Always check egg rarity before hatching — a Golden Egg with an Exceptional nature is potentially worth building your entire team around.

    Splicing Station: The Endgame System

    Unlocked later in the game, the Splicing Station lets you mix and match genes across different species. This is the ultimate customization tool:

    • Element fusion: Create dual-element Voidlings (e.g., Fire + Ice for shatter combos)
    • Ability transfer: Move a powerful ability from one Voidling to another
    • Stat boosting: Overwrite a Voidling's weak stat with a donor's strong stat
    • Custom appearance: Mix body parts, colors, and patterns from different gene pools

    ⚠️ Splicing Is Expensive

    Splicing consumes rare resources and the donor Voidling is permanently lost. Only splice when you're confident the result is a significant upgrade. Never splice your only copy of a rare Voidling.

    Optimal Breeding Strategy: Step by Step

    1. Step 1: Catch multiple of the same species. Compare stats — keep the top 2 for breeding.
    2. Step 2: Breed the best male + best female. Hatch the egg. Check the offspring's Nature.
    3. Step 3: If the offspring is better than the parents, replace the weaker parent with it. Repeat.
    4. Step 4: Aim for Exceptional Nature + desired mutation perks. This takes many breeding cycles.
    5. Step 5: Once you have a "perfect" Voidling, use the Training Room to passively level it while you continue hunting.
    6. Step 6: Late game: use the Splicing Station to add an element or ability from a different species.

    Training Room: AFK Leveling

    The Training Room lets you assign Voidlings to passively gain XP while you use others on missions. Always keep your breeding projects and backup Voidlings in training:

    • Assign Voidlings you're leveling for evolution
    • Train freshly hatched offspring to catch them up quickly
    • Rotate between 2-3 training slots for maximum efficiency
