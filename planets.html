🌍 Voidling Bound Planets: Vireo, Solum & Aulis

📅 Updated: June 9, 2026🎮 Core Mechanics⏱️ ~5 min read

Known Planets

Based on the official Playtest and Steam description, Voidling Bound features handcrafted planets oozing with diverse enemies, each posing unique gameplay challenges. Three planets were accessible during the extended playtest:

PlanetEnvironmentKnown Features
🌿 VireoLush, overgrownFirst planet in story; introductory missions; basic enemy types
☀️ SolumArid, harshMid-game planet; tougher enemies; rare resources
🌑 AulisCorrupted, darkLate-story planet; high-level enemies; rare eggs and mutagens

📝 Source Note

Planet names Vireo, Solum, and Aulis are confirmed from the official Playtest announcement by Hatchery Games. Full planet count and post-launch additions TBD.

Planet Progression

The official description mentions "cleansing vibrant handcrafted planets" suggesting a story-driven progression through worlds. Completing missions on each planet yields:

  • Resources: materials for crafting and upgrades
  • Research Points: used in the tech/research tree
  • Voidling Eggs: hatch new Voidlings unique to each biome
  • Mutagens: key items for fighting the parasite

What to Expect Per Planet

Vireo — The Starting World

Your first destination. Introduces basic combat, Voidling collection, and mission structure. Enemy types are simpler—focus on learning your Voidling's moveset here.

Solum — The Mid-Game Hurdle

Environments become more hostile. Enemies hit harder and require element-type matchups. This is where attribute allocation and team composition start to matter.

Aulis — The Corrupted Endgame

The most dangerous known planet. Heavy corruption, rare Voidling eggs, and high-tier mutagens. Bring your best team and be ready for boss fights.

Galactic Map & Navigation

The official description mentions a Galactic Map interface (also used to access the Abyss). From here you select planets, missions, and game modes.
